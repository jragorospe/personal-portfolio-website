A Musical Experiment

Ardui-bone

ðŸ”— Github Link

Created a trombone prototype using an Arduino, an ultrasonic sensor, a gyroscope, an air pressure sensor, a buzzer, and scraps I found in my garage and programmed it using C++ to behave like a traditional trombone. Calculates slide position using the ultrasonic sensor, determines volume with the air pressure sensor, and changes octaves using pitch calculated by the gyroscope.

A little bit about me...

I'm a Canadian developer based in Calgary, AB with a B.Sc of Computer Science and a minor of Philosophy from the University of Calgary. I specialized in Human Computer Interactions, exploring new ways to innovate in the interactive media medium.

I love games! From gaming on the NES to the Switch, modding to Game Jams, and finally working in the industry, my obsession for everything that goes into games knows no bounds. I'm only just getting my start but I hope to make my mark on the industry someday.
I am passionate about serving others and promoting ways to act intentionally and sustainably with our lives, exploring the ways people think through the study of philosophy, psychology and design. When I'm not coding you can find me climbing at my local gym, getting destroyed in the local Smash Ultimate scene, or tooting my trombone in the CSC Orchestra.
